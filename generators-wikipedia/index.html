    Wikipedia (customblocks.generates.wikipedia)

    Generates a linkcard from from a lemma for the Wikipedia.

    The difference with a linkcard is that instead of the full url to the article you just must provide the lemma/title.

    Options

    lemma
    The lemma for the article
    lang (keyword only, default en)
    Language (or wikipedia instance)
    *args
    extra positional arguments are appended as classes for the top level element
    **kwds
    any extra keyword argument is used like in a linkcard

    Examples

    ::: wikipedia "Sant Joan Despí"

    Sant Joan Despí - Wikipedia

    Sant Joan Despí (Old Catalan for 'Saint John of the Pine'; Catalan pronunciation: [ˈsaɲ ʒuˈan dəsˈpi]) is a city and municipality located in the Baix Llobregat area (Barcelona province in Catalonia, Spain). It is situated on the left bank of the Llobregat river.

    Styling

    Generated Wikipedia cards are just linkcards with an additional wikipedia class to target them specifically, if you need to. Refer to linkcard styling section. Examples above use the stylesheet referred there.
